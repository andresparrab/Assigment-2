Social impact in society and etic, integrity


Well, what can i say here, internet is a part of almost everything. And it has a big impact in society.

to answer this questions. I will talk about an own example from my own experience. I have made my whole home like a smart home. for example when I am five minutes from getting home, google will know this by getting my GPS location and start the coffee machine. So when I arrive home I am welcome with a pleasant coffee smell. When I go to sleep I say goodnight and the tv in the living room, speakers and everything will turn off. Then ask me for what time to set the alarm for the next day.

When I run I also use run keeper app for track my speed, calories etc. And the apps want me to stay on track if I miss running some days, or that now that I have run so many km then I should buy Asics tuning shoes...etc

But in this case I am aware of how the people that program the sites try to to make me buy this brand shoes or guilt-trip me if I dint run enough this week or not burn enough calories and I can see how they want some some kind of control of my life. And because this awareness I dont live and breathe about what my running app tells me to do, or what netflix suggest me to watch or what product I is the perfect for me, or what news to see. Of course I dont say I dont follow any suggestions or so in the apps, or browser or computer. But I am aware of what company want with this website, if the product of this free site/app is ME (because nothing is really free, and a lot of information about me my integrity get passed around) and then I try to make an informed decisions. Do I still fall into traps that the internet companies want me to? sure, I am only human.

It is very important to have awareness about internet and that you how they program devices, apps and websites and what they really want us to do and be critic. Also is important to know that we can change the thing in internet , they are not almighty and that WE can change what information we want or not by taking action and have a voice out there.

The examples above are just few things.. there are devices connectet to the internet everywhere around us. from smart watches, toothbrushes with a.i that suggest you how to brush your teeth, smart lights, of course computers and phones, smart tv:s that always can show you what you should see. weight scales, that can tell you body fat(not accurate) weight, pulse etc, send this to your phone and then show you your progress or opposite. The list are endless.
